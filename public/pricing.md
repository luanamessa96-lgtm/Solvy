# Solvy — Pricing

## Free Trial
- Duration: 7 days
- Credit card: not required
- Access: full Pro features during trial
- Countries: Spain, Italy

## Pro Monthly
- Price: €7.99/month
- Billing: monthly, cancel anytime
- Features:
  - Unlimited invoices
  - Real-time IVA and IRPF calculation
  - Modelo 303 generation (Spain)
  - Fiscal calendar with deadline alerts
  - Income and expense tracking
  - Annual tax summary (Modelo 390)
  - PWA — iOS, Android, web

## Pro Yearly
- Price: €59.99/year (equivalent to €5.00/month)
- Saving: 37% vs monthly plan
- Billing: annual, one payment
- Features: same as Pro Monthly

## Free Plan
- Available: No — Solvy offers a 7-day free trial, not a permanent free tier

## Notes
- All plans include Spain (autónomos) and Italy (Regime Forfettario / Ordinario)
- No accountant (gestor) needed — calculations are automated
- Prices include VAT where applicable
